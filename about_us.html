We are
Mojo 365 Hub.

Established in 2019 in Bangalore, India, we are a passionate team of experts specializing in Microsoft 365 and Power Platform solutions. Our core focus is empowering organizations to maximize their potential through enhanced communication, collaboration, and productivity, while elevating employee experiences through streamlined processes.

With pride in our proficiency, we excel in designing, developing, and implementing successful Intranet and portal solutions, utilizing SharePoint and Teams. Additionally, we are skilled in writing apps and automating custom business applications.

Embracing a customer-centric approach and unwavering commitment to excellence, we are the ideal partner for organizations seeking a competitive edge in today's dynamic business landscape.

Mission: Empowering organizations with Microsoft 365 cloud solutions for enhanced productivity, collaboration, and employee experience.

Vision: Leading digital transformation, fostering connected and thriving enterprises through cutting-edge Microsoft 365 innovations.

Our values — through the years, we have been shaped and formed by both successes and failures.

1 — Challenge core assumptions

This idea remains the starting point for everything we do. We unlock new solutions by challenging both our client’s — and our own — assumptions about a product, an audience, and what is possible. This idea was born out of the very foundation of our agency. Many assumed we could not run a successful digital agency from a Croatian Island — we continue to challenge this particular assumption every single day.

2 — Learning through doing

We approach every problem with an open mind, and sometimes finding the best solutions means taking the road less traveled. We’re not afraid to experiment, to try new things, or make mistakes along the way. Learning through doing keeps us sharp.

3 — Focus and determination

We carefully consider every project we take on because when we commit, we always bring 110%. If we see a way to make something smarter, faster, better, we’ll always push for it (even when it makes our job that bit harder!). We’re driven by a desire to build great experiences shaped by data, and sometimes that means going above and beyond the brief.

4 — Listen smart

We take an open-door approach to communication, with both our clients and each other. Everyone’s feedback is valuable, and we’re never too busy to hear it. Listening smart is not just hearing, it’s taking the time to truly understand, without ego, and be willing and ready to adapt.

We’re on a mission to make what is digital more human.

Living in a digital age, people’s experiences can sometimes feel quite disconnected. That is why our work values the human connection and always has users as the focal point.
